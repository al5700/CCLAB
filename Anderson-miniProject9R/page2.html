The Raven by Edgar Allan Poe - Poem Analysis
The Raven by Edgar Allan Poe This popular
narrative poem is written in the first person.
‘ The Raven ‘ personifies the feeling of intense grief and
loss, while other symbols throughout the poem reinforce a melodramatic
mood that emphasizes the main character’s grief and loss.
