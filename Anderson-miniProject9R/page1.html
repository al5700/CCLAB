Edgar Allan's Biography

Edgar Allan Poe ( / poʊ /; born Edgar Poe; January 19, 1809 – October 7, 1849)
was an American writer, poet, editor, and literary critic. Poe is best
known for his poetry and short stories, particularly his tales of mystery and
the macabre. He is widely regarded as a central figure of Romanticism
in the United States, and of American literature..
